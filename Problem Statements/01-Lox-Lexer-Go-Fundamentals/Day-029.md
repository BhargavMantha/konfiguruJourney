## üí° Key Learning Points

### Why Buffer Days Matter

**Benefits of buffer days:**
1. Catch unfinished work without stress
2. Prevent feeling rushed
3. Allow for unexpected life events
4. Provide mental recovery time
5. Ensure quality over speed

### Sustainable Learning

**Month 1 was a test:**
- Can you sustain 13.5 hrs/week?
- Is the schedule working?
- Are you still enjoying it?
- Do you need adjustments?

**For 23 more months to succeed:**
- Must be sustainable
- Must be enjoyable
- Must fit your life
- Must leave room for life

### The Importance of Celebration

**Why celebrate small wins:**
- Reinforces progress
- Builds motivation
- Creates positive associations
- Honors your effort
- Maintains momentum

**You deserve to celebrate!** Building a lexer from scratch is a real achievement!
